General Safety Guidelines

Verify Profiles: Check user ratings, reviews, and completed tasks before accepting or posting a task.

Communicate on Platform: Keep all conversations within Workmate4u for your safety and record-keeping.

Meet in Public: For first meetings, choose well-lit public places like cafes or malls.

Tell Someone: Inform a friend or family member about your task location and expected time.

Payment Safety

Never pay outside the platform. All legitimate payments go through Workmate4u's secure Razorpay gateway.

No advance payments. Don't send money before the task is completed unless it's through our escrow system.

  • Don't share bank details directly with other users
  • Be wary of unusually high-paying tasks
  • Report suspicious payment requests immediately

Red Flags to Watch For

Requests for personal info: Aadhar, PAN, passwords, OTPs

Pressure to act quickly: "Urgent! Send money now!"

Moving off-platform: "Let's chat on WhatsApp instead"

Too good to be true: Extremely high pay for simple tasks

For Task Posters

  • Write clear, detailed task descriptions
  • Set realistic budgets and timelines
  • Check Tasker reviews before accepting their offer
  • Confirm identity when meeting in person
  • Inspect work before confirming completion

For Taskers

  • Read task descriptions carefully before applying
  • Clarify expectations before starting work
  • Trust your instincts - decline tasks that feel wrong
  • Document your work with photos when appropriate
  • Report problematic Task Posters
