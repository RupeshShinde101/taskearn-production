Sign Up & Verify
Getting Started

How to Sign Up & Verify Your Email

Create your free Workmate4u account in under a minute. Learn how to fill in your details and verify your email with OTP.

  • Click "Sign Up" on the homepage
  • Enter your name, email, phone & password
  • Enter the 6-digit OTP sent to your email
  • Start using Workmate4u instantly
2 min Beginner
Post a Task
Posting Tasks

How to Post a Task

Learn how to post a task with the right title, description, budget, and location to get the best helpers nearby.

  • Click "Post a Task" and choose a category
  • Write a clear title and description
  • Set your budget (min ₹100) and location
  • Submit — your task is live for 12 hours
3 min Beginner
Find & Accept Tasks
Earning Money

How to Find & Accept Tasks

Browse tasks on the map, filter by category and distance, and accept tasks to start earning money right away.

  • Go to "Find Tasks" or "Browse Tasks"
  • Enable location to see tasks near you on the map
  • Filter by category, distance, and budget
  • Tap a task and click "Accept" to start working
3 min Beginner
Wallet & Payments
Payments

How to Use the Wallet & Payments

Top up your wallet via UPI/card, pay for tasks securely, and track all your transactions in one place.

  • Open "Wallet" from the navigation menu
  • Click "Add Money" and pay via UPI, card, or net banking
  • Wallet balance is used for task payments & service charges
  • View full transaction history anytime

Earning Money

How to Earn Money on Workmate4u

Tips and strategies to maximize your earnings — from picking the right tasks to building a 5-star reputation.

  • Accept tasks that match your skills and location
  • Complete tasks on time to build a strong rating
  • Earnings are credited directly to your wallet
  • Refer friends with your code to earn bonus rewards

Stay Safe
Safety

Safety Tips for Users

Essential safety guidelines to protect yourself while posting tasks or working as a tasker on Workmate4u.

  • Always use in-app payments — never pay in cash
  • Meet in public places for task handoffs
  • Check ratings and reviews before accepting
  • Report suspicious users immediately
