Suggested Implementations for AI Team & Admin Panel

AI Auto-Resolution

Allow AI agent to automatically resolve low-risk disputes (e.g. auto-refund for tasks stuck in-progress for 7+ days without update) and only escalate high-risk disputes to human admins.

Real-time Analytics

Add live charts showing: dispute rate trend, AI agent accuracy, fraud detection hit rate, top problem categories, and daily resolution time average.

Admin Notifications

Push or email alerts when: a new Critical AI report is posted, a dispute has been open >24h, a user gets multiple flags in one day, or a settlement request needs approval.

User Risk Score

Show a calculated risk score per user (based on # disputes filed, # tasks flagged, # reports received) so admins can spot bad actors at a glance and proactively act.

AI Agent History per Task

Show a timeline of every AI action on a specific task (flagged → reviewed → restored/removed) so admins understand the full history without digging through audit logs.

Report Export

Let admins export AI reports, disputes, and audit logs as CSV/Excel for compliance, legal review, or sharing with stakeholders — with date range filtering.
