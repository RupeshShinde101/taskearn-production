1. Introduction

Workmate4u ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform.

Your Privacy Matters: We only collect information necessary to provide our services and never sell your personal data to third parties.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

Data Type Purpose
Name Account identification, communication
Email Address Account login, notifications, support
Phone Number Account verification, task communication
Date of Birth Age verification (16+ requirement)
Password Account security (stored encrypted)
Payment Information Processed by Razorpay (not stored by us)

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, device type
  • Location Data: With your permission, for task matching
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, time spent
  • IP Address: For security and analytics

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Connect you with Task Posters or Taskers
  • Process payments through Razorpay
  • Send important notifications and updates
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Detect and prevent fraud
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Information Sharing

We may share your information with:

4.1 Other Users

  • Your name and rating are visible to other users
  • Contact details shared only after task acceptance

4.2 Service Providers

  • Razorpay: Payment processing
  • SendGrid: Email notifications
  • Cloud Providers: Data storage and hosting

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government request.

We Never Sell Your Data: Your personal information is never sold to advertisers or third-party marketers.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted via HTTPS/TLS
  • Password Hashing: Passwords stored using PBKDF2-SHA256
  • Access Controls: Limited employee access to personal data
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and updates

6. Data Retention

Data Type Retention Period
Account Information Until account deletion + 30 days
Task History 5 years (legal/tax requirements)
Payment Records 7 years (financial regulations)
Support Tickets 3 years

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw Consent: Opt-out of marketing communications

To exercise these rights, contact us at info@workmate4u.com

8. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies for:

  • Keeping you logged in
  • Remembering your preferences
  • Analytics and performance monitoring

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

9. Third-Party Links

Our platform may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their privacy policies.

10. Children's Privacy

Workmate4u is not intended for users under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If we discover such data, it will be deleted immediately.

11. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than India. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via email or platform notification. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
