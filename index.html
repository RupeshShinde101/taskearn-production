FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about India's leading freelance marketplace

What is Workmate4u?

Workmate4u is India's leading freelance marketplace that connects people who need tasks done with skilled freelancers nearby. You can hire freelancers for delivery, cleaning, tutoring, repairs, photography and more — all across major Indian cities.

How do I hire a freelancer on Workmate4u?

Sign up for free, post your task with a description and budget, and local freelancers will accept your task. Track progress in real-time and pay securely through Razorpay.

How much can I earn as a freelancer?

Earnings depend on the tasks you complete. Freelancers earn between ₹50 to ₹10,000+ per task depending on complexity and distance. Top earners make over ₹40,000 per month on Workmate4u.

Is Workmate4u free to join?

Yes, creating an account on Workmate4u is completely free. A small service charge is deducted only when a task is completed successfully.

Which cities does Workmate4u operate in?

Workmate4u operates across all major Indian cities including Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Pune, Chennai, Kolkata, Ahmedabad, Jaipur, and many more. Any location in India with internet access is supported.

Is Workmate4u safe and secure?

Yes. All payments are processed through Razorpay (PCI-DSS compliant). Users are verified, and we have a rating system, KYC verification, and safety guidelines to protect both clients and freelancers.
